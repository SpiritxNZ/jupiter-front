Upon receipt of your booking enquiry, we will determine the availability of your selected items for your chosen date, and contact you to confirm. To secure your booking, a 50% deposit is payable within 3 days of your acceptance. Final payment is due before you pick up/delivery day.

A bond is payable on all hire items, starting at $100, calculated depending on the value of items hired. Bonds are fully refundable on return of items in original condition minus fair wear and tear.

The standard hire period on all items is 3 days.
No minimum spending is required if you hire on weekends (you must return products by Monday).
The minimum spending is $50 if you hire between Monday and Friday. Hire pickup time: every Friday 10:00-2:00. Time to return the products: 10:00-2:00 on Monday. Please let us know if you wish to pick up outside of those times.
Please note: Prices listed on the website include GST.
