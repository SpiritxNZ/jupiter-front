PAYMENT DETAILS

PAYMENT
  1. All rental item prices are inclusive of 15% GST .
  2. Customers are asked to make all payments to Luxe Dream Event Hire via bank transfer. Bank details will be provided on the individual customer invoice.
  3. The remaining balance is to be paid on or before the day of item collection and/or delivery.
  4. Additional delivery charges may apply, and these will be provided on the individual customer invoice.
  5. Should the customer be given a discount, this will be stated on the individual customer invoice.

DEPOSIT
  1. Customers are required to provide a deposit to secure their item[s], which is calculated at 50% per rented item, which is inclusive of GST.
  2. The deposit due date is provided on the individual customer invoice.
  3. On payment of the deposit, Luxe Dream Event Hire will keep an item available for the customer for their specified event date


BOND
  1. Bond calculation as percentage of the total rent (before any discounts). Customers are asked to provide a minimum bond of $150.00 NZD and maximum bond setting at $500.00 NZD. Within $250.00 NZD, the bond will be $150.00 NZD; between $250.00 NZD and $750.00 the bond would be $300.00 NZD; ABOVE $750.00 NZD, the bond would be $500.00 NZD.
  2. The bond price may vary depending on the item or quantity of items the customer wishes to hire. The bond price will be outlined on each individual invoice.
  3. The bond will be returned within three working days given the item[s] has/have been returned and inspected to ensure that it/they has/have not been soiled or damaged.

CANCELLATION POLICY

CUSTOMER CANCELLATION
Email: info@luxedreameventhire.co.nz
If the customer wishes to cancel hiring their rental item[s], they must inform Luxe Dream Event Hire as soon as possible, in writing, in order to be eligible for a refund. Refunds are as follows:
  1. If the customer cancels three weeks before the collection/delivery date, they will be provided with: a full refund.
  2. If the customer cancels between one to three weeks before the collection/delivery date, they will be provided with: a refund of 50% of their deposit.
  3. If the customer cancels less than one week before the collection/delivery date, they will be provided with: no refund.

LUXE DREAMS CANCELLATION
  1. Luxe Dream Event Hire reserves the right to cancel item collection/delivery should they consider that the customer is unsuitable to take care of the item[s] properly.
  2. Luxe Dream Event Hire reserves the right to cancel item collection/delivery should a previous customer return the item in a damaged condition, leaving the item unfit for the next customer.

RESCHEDULING OF HIRE DATE
  1. There is no charge to the customer for changing the date of their event, on the basis that Luxe Dreams have the item[s] available on the new date.
  2. Luxe Dreams must be informed 3 days prior to the collection/delivery date if the customer wishes to change their collection/delivery date.
  3. Customers may change the date of their event up to three times.


CARE OF ITEMS


CLEANING
  1. Luxe Dream Event Hire items are expected to be returned in the same condition as they were collected/delivered.
  2. All items must be cleaned properly before returning to Luxe Dream Event Hire.
SOILED AND/OR DAMAGED ITEMS
  1. Customers will be charged 25% of their bond if an item is returned in a soiled condition.
  2. Customers will be charged part or all of their bond if an item is returned damaged. The bond deduction will be determined by Luxe Dream Event Hire, depending on the extent of the damage.
  3. Customers must inform Luxe Dream Event Hire if they are aware of any damage to an item.
  4. Any lost Luxe Dream Event Hire items will be charged at a replacement cost and is to the cost of the customer.
